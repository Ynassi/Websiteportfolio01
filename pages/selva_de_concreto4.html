SELVA DE CONCRETO : FORTH EDITION

Despite some great experiences working with the Aussenwelt team, we decided to find a new venue for the 4th edition of Selva de Concreto. We secured a warehouse-style rental space in the Saint-Henri district of Montreal that aligned perfectly with our artistic vision. For this edition, we went all out. The lineup included jazz bands, rappers, singers, poets, tattoo artists, muralists, and live painting sessions. The venue featured multiple spaces—a warehouse area for graffiti artists, a stage area for concerts, and an outdoor space with a barbecue. Selva 4 stood out from previous editions by offering the public the opportunity to get involved in the creative process. Several artistic workshops were available to participants, allowing them to discover their inner artist. This edition marked the culmination of years of work and refinement of our artistic direction, and it far exceeded our expectations in terms of ticket sales and atmosphere.

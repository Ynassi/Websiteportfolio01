SELVA DE CONCRETO : FIRST EDITION

Selva de Concreto is a biennial non-profit participatory event whose mission is to bring together artists from different artistic fields in order to give them a common visibility and outreach for their work. We had a nice barbecue, an art exhibition and a hip-hop show in the evening. Some people were also here to sell merch, clothing and jewelry. This was the first event of the Selva the Concreto, we didn't expect to have that much good feedback. This event took place at the beggining of the summer and end of the pandemic, everyone wanted to go out, share, and come together which naturally created an incredible vibe.

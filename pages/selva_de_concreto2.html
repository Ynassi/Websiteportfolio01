SELVA DE CONCRETO : SECOND EDITION

For its second edition, Selva de Concreto took place at Aussenwelt, a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting local art. We also had the pleasure of collaborating with La Pingouinerie, a French-Montreal DJ collective known for bringing their signature quirky and energetic vibe. We kept the winning formula from the first event—combining visual art with the world of hip-hop. Our exhibitors and projection artists were once again an essential part of the experience.

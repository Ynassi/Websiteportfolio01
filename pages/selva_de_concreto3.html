SELVA DE CONCRETO : THIRD EDITION

For its third edition, Selva de Concreto had the privilege of being one of the first events to take place in Aussenwelt’s new space at the Union Française de Montréal. This marked our debut in a venue of such scale. We tripled our capacity, and with an effective marketing strategy, we sold nearly all available tickets. The event was a resounding success, meeting our expectations and elevating us to the next level thanks to the valuable advice from several members of the Aussenwelt team.
